Why now

You're not just buying into a property.

You're tapping into a touchstone of our culture. That can be very powerful.

Yet no one owns Wonderland — not because it's public domain, but because it has never been centralized. The way Michael Crichton owns dinosaurs: you need Jurassic Park.

But you need a story. A way in.

One that connects the dots for a new generation. For decades to come . . .

The Looking Glass Wars
is that story, brought to life today.
